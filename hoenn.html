Hoenn

The Hoenn region is west of sinnoh and south west of kanto and johto.
It is based on real life kyushu islands of Japan. Professor of this region is Professor birch who gives the player the choice of The three starters in new bark town. The region is unique as its mostly divided into two equal parts, the mainland which is to the west and the mostly small island in vast ocean which makes up the east. Most Settlements in this region are named after combination of two words.

Starters

Mudkip

Mud fish Pokémon

The fin on MUDKIP’s head acts as highly sensitive radar. Using this fin to sense movements of water and air, this POKéMON can determine what is taking place around it.

Torchic

Chick Pokémon

TORCHIC sticks with its TRAINER, following behind with unsteady steps. This POKéMON breathes fire of over 1,800 degrees F that leave the foe scorched black.

Treecko

Wood gecko POKéMON

TREECKO has small hooks on the bottom of its feet that enable it to scale vertical walls. This POKéMON attacks by slamming foes with its thick tail.

Notable Locations

Mount chimney is a volcanic mountain in north west part of the region. it is connected by a cable car which can take you to the top its thermal energy is used for hot springs in Lavaridge. the volcanic ashes sometimes covers the lower region with soot.

Granite cave is located on the island of Dewford Town. The cave is extremely had to see in due to darkness. at the end of the cave there is an ancient mural.

Battle frontier is a city that offers 5 unique challenges to the trainers. it is based on a island which means it can only be accesed through a boat from Slateport or lilycove city.

Sky pillar is a massive tower which is extremely hard to traverse on its top the legandary pokemon rayquaza can be found.
