Kanto

The Kanto region is based on real life region of the same name in Japan, it is located east of Johto which is located south of sinnoh
The Professor of this region is Professor Oak who gives the player the choice The three starters. it is made up of mostly grasslands. The league in this region is called "The indigo league"

Starters

Squirtle

Tiny turtle pokemon

After birth, its back swells and hardens into a shell. Powerfully sprays foam from its mouth

Charmander

Lizard Pokémon

The flame at the tip of its tail makes a sound as it burns. You can only hear it in quiet places.

Bulbasaur

Seed POKéMON

A strange seed was planted on its back at birth. The plant sprouts and grows with this POKéMON.

Pikachu

Mouse POKéMON

When several of these POKéMON gather, their electricity could build and cause lightning storms.

Eevee

Evolution POKéMON

Its genetic code is unstable, so it could evolve in a variety of ways. There are only a few alive.

Notable Locations

MT moon is a location in northern Kanto. Between pewter City and Cerulean it have a complex cave system which is used to find moon stones which is he cave named after and fossils. Mount moon is of the the rare locations where clefairy can be found.

Celadon game corner is an arcade in celadon city that is ruan by team rocket. It leads to a secret underground team rocket hideout by pressing the button behind the poster

Silph Co head office is headquarter of company by the same name which is located in saffron city. This company is responsible for making variety of goods including silph scope and master ball. It is taken over by team rocket when the player reaches it.

Indigo plateau is the place where pokemon league takes place in kanto. trainers who have collected all 8 badges can challenge the elite 4 and champion here.
